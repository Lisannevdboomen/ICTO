Submit final grades to SIS (or retake grades)

 

1.

 

Open Final Grades from the course menu

Wait for it to load, it may take some time.

 

2.

Select for whom you want to submit grades

If you are the examiner of the whole course, select All students.

 

If you are the examiner of a certain section, select Section.

If you would like to select certain students, then use the red boxes in front of students' names.

 
3. Click the Submit grades button at the right side of the page
 

 
4. Select Final grade or Retake grade from the dropdown box at the top and press Submit.
 

 
5. A pop-up window will open, after which you'll be redirected to a new window to start the 2FA process and sign the gradelist.
 

(Please make sure your browser enables pop-ups.) 

If you're not automatically redirected to the 2FA process, open the page manually.

    What is Two Factor Authentication (2FA)?

 
6.

Sign the gradelist with 2FA, using your Tiqr-app (or YubiKey)
 

   
7.

Confirmation and optional download of grade list
 

The gradelist has been signed successfully when the following message appears:

"This grade list has been signed and confirmed."

 

 

If desired, download the grade list in the form of a PDF file.

 
8.

You're done! Click the Final grades button menu to reload the page
 

The gradelist will now display symbols in front of students' names. You do not need to act on these, the administration office will contact you when you do.
 

 = The final grade is currently being transferred to SIS.

= The final grade has been successfully transferred to SIS.

 = The resit grade has been successfully transferred to SIS.

= You can no longer submit grades to SIS for this student because (a) they've successfully completed the course, or (b) both the final grade and resit grade have been submitted to SIS.

 =  You cannot transfer grades for this student

 =  This student is not enrolled in SIS for this course.

=  This grade was imported from a file or entered or modified manually.

= Unfortunately, an error occurred during the transfer. You don't have to act on this error! The administration office will automatically receive a message and they will contact you if anything seems wrong. In most cases, this icon only indicates errors that are excepted.
