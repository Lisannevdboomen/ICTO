By embedding the weblecture in Canvas, only students who are enrolled to your Canvas course can watch the recording. Moreover, the weblecture recording cannot be downloaded if you follow the steps below. If you want to add extra restrictions and details (such as restricting a time limit and/or sharing the recording with a few students) then create an empty "Assignment" instead of a "Page". This will provide you with extra options.

How to embed weblectures in Canvas


1. Go to the page or assignment where you want to add the video and click Edit.
   
2. Click on the Mediasite icon (4 orange dots).
 


3. Select Presentation.
  By selecting "Collection" you can add all the lectures at once, with "Presentation" you're only sharing one particular lecture


4. Search for the specific weblecture by its subject name or code.
 


5. Click on the button Add to.. next to the video and then select Player Only.
  The 'Player Only' option will embed the video.


6. Once you're done, click on Save & Publish at the bottom of the page.
  Or click 'Save' if you do not want the video to be published yet.
