About the Thesis Workflow

The thesis courses in Canvas have been using the thesis workflow since 2019, and this guide explains what it entails exactly and which points are especially important to thesis coordinators and examiners. The thesis workflow has been created to ensure that a student's master thesis can be published to the online library (scripties.uba.uva) when the thesis has been finished.

The workflow verifies that all conditions of the course have been met and all approvals have been obtained. Once these requirements are met, the system automatically sends the thesis to the UvA library for publication. We therefore request that you leave all items in the Thesis Workflow module in their exact order and do not change the module settings. Preferably keep the Assignments menu closed, do not edit or delete the assignments, and please do not create an alternative assignment for submitting the final thesis.

Thesis supervisors are responsible for grading the thesis (interpret plagiarism score, enter student's grade, and upload the thesis assessment form) and for approving the publication of the thesis.

 

Grading the student’s thesis in Canvas (thesis supervisor) 

Once you have determined the final grade after filling out the assessment form and consultation with the second (or third, or fourth…) examiner you will have to add this to the assignment. You can do this using SpeedGrader. Here you'll have to add both the final grade and the assessment form. It is also possible for you to view, check, comment on, or download the thesis. Set up a thesis deadline

If you want to set a deadline on the (2/3) Submit final version or Retake assignment for 1 or more students; use the Assign To function in the assignment settings. You can click on the + Add button to add a new item. Then, add the student name in the Assign To column and enter a Due date. Finally, click Save at the bottom of the page. This way, all instructors within the canvas course can use their own deadlines for students and no one gets in each other's way in the thesis workflow process.

  

 

Approving the publication of the thesis (thesis supervisor)

    Yes, this thesis may be published

If you give your approval for the publication of the thesis and the student has met all other conditions, then you will not need to take any further steps for this part.

    No, publication of this thesis needs to be blocked

If you do not want the thesis to be published, go to the assignment called (3/3) Publish thesis to the UBA in the Thesis Workflow module. In this tool, you'll have to give permission for the publication tool to read information from Canvas. After this a list of students will appear (see image below). Please check the box next to the student in the column Block publication if you'd like to prevent that thesis from being published. This list is available from the start of the course.
