MyMedia

 

 
  • Kaltura
  • Share videos
  • Student upload

Kaltura is a modern video platform. It is a durable state-of-the-art solution for or creating video or audio by teachers, supporters and students. Kaltura works very well with Canvas, you can find it under the name 'MyMedia'. As a teacher you can easily offer videos within courses (the storage of videos has no effect on course memory).

Zoom

 

 
  • Zoom
  • Canvas Integration
  • Breakout rooms

Zoom is a cloud-based video conference service that you can use to virtually meet with your students - either by video, audio-only, or both - while conducting live chats. It also allows you to record sessions to view later. In light of the COVID-19 pandemic, the University of Amsterdam has moved to digital teaching. In order to support teachers during these times, the University has made Zoom available to all staff and students on short notice. In particular, for teaching, staff and students can now find Zoom embedded into Canvas.
