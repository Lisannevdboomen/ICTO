What is TestVision?

TestVision is testing software that serves the examination process of online exams. It is a comprehensive testing system that supports you in developing, administering, grading, and analyzing tests. We recommend the use of TestVision because:

  • There will be technical guidance present during the exam. Therefore, students aren't able to plagiarize or commit fraud during the exam.
  • In creating the exam, teachers only have to edit questions and no longer have to deal with all the technical aspects of a test. Questions can easily be changed by the teacher until the last moment, provided that no major changes are being made. For instance, when new questions need to be added or the entire structure of the exam needs to change you will need to communicate these changes with ICTO at least 1 week before the exam.
  • All results will directly be visible within your Final Grades menu: teachers no longer need to work with an export or import of grades.

 

Book an appointment with us!

We have heard from teachers that an (online or onsite) appointment of 15-20 minutes works best: there is time to ask questions, to pass on changes and wishes regarding the test, and to go through the question menu in TestVision together with ICTO. A meeting with ICTO gives teachers some security and we often hear: "Actually, it's not that bad". So please do not hesitate to drop by our office, email us or book an appointment. Take a look at our contact page to get in touch with us.

Applying for TestVision

If you want to do an online exam or inspection with TestVision (using technical support), notify your programme coordinator as early as possible. Programme coordinators are in charge of scheduling the exam or inspection and sending a request for TestVision support to ICTO. Thereafter, our team must book in all details according to the roster at least 7 weeks before the start of the exam, retake or inspection.

The following deadlines apply:

  • Exams or inspections in block 1 --- August, 25 (2023)
  • Exams or inspections in block 2+3 --- October, 6 (2023)
  • Exams or inspections in block 4 --- January, 12 (2024)
  • Exams or inspections in block 5+6 --- March, 8 (2024)

 

What do I need to do?

The ICTO team sets up a copy of last year's exam with copies of last year's questions and test settings – or if this is your first time, we'll set up concept questions and assign basic settings to the exam.

Teachers are solely responsible for modifying the questions in TestVision. Questions can be changed by teachers until the last moment. A request for adding new questions to the test or changing test settings can be sent to ICTO up to one week before the start of the test. After this deadline, teachers can also do these things themselves with the help of these instructions.
