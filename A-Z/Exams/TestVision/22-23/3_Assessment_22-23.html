1.1   Getting started with grading in TestVision

1. Go to the Grading menu.
 

2. Go to the Grader tab and click on Grade.
 


3. Start grading by clicking on Grade next to the desired student. You can also choose to grade by question, by going to the Grade per question tab.
 

4. A new window will open in which you can assign points based on the criteria of a question. When you're done grading a question, click on Next to go the next question.
 


5. When you've graded all students, make sure to click on Validate to generate the grades based on the points you've assigned.
