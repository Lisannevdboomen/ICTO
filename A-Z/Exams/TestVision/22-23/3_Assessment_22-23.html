1.1   How to grade in TestVision

  • Step-by-step
  • Video
TestVision Help: ENG | NL

 

Note: Only open questions need to be graded, as multiple choice questions are graded automatically.

1. Go to the Grading menu.
 

Grading menu

2. Go to the Grader tab and click on Grade.
  The grader tab will display the exams to which you've been assigned as grader. Don't see your exam(s)?


3. Start grading by clicking on Grade next to the desired student.
  You can also choose to grade by question, by going to the Grade per question tab.
4. Assign points.
  When you're done grading a question, click on Next to go the next question (or student, if you're grading per question).

1.2   Validate the grades

  • Step-by-step
  • Video
TestVision Help: ENG | NL

 

Note: Please make sure all students are graded before performing this step.

1. Go to the Grading menu.
 

Grading menu

2. Go to the Grader tab and click on Grade.
  The grader tab will display the exams to which you've been assigned as grader. Don't see your exam(s)?


3. Validate your grades.
  Click on the top-right Validate button to confirm that you're done grading. You can also validate individual students' exams by clicking validate behind their name.
 

 
Note: only the course coordinator(s) can perform the actions displayed in below's video.

View the instruction video 'Coordinating the grading process' for a short introduction on the grading process.
