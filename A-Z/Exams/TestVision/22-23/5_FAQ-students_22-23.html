I have a disability and need extra time for the exam. What should I do?

Please visit this page to read the most current information about extra time. Beware that you need to start asking for extra time at least a month prior to the exam because it may take a little while to be processed.

What do I need to bring to the exam?

You need to bring your UvA Pass and identification. You also need to enter your UvAnetID and password in TestVision to login.

 

 Back to top

Can I bring a bag? Are there lockers or something to leave things?

Students can bring bags and just place them under their table.

How do I login to the exam?

If you are going to take the exam on location, there will be technical staff present to guide you to the login process. If you are going to take the exam from home (online exam), please view this instruction sheet (short PDF guide). You need to login to TestVision with your UvAnetID and password.

What happens if I’m late? Can I nevertheless start the exam?

If a student is 30 minutes late (or later), they cannot enter the exam room. Also: students can only leave the exam room after 30 minutes, not earlier.

 

 Back to top

What if I do not have extra time during the exam even though I am entitled to it?

Please raise your hand as soon as possible to inform the teachers or staff about it. You are too late to get extra time if you have already closed the exam (at the end) so double check the timer that is present in your screen at the very start of the exam (or during). You will be given a temporary non-personal account to take the exam with extra time. Please note that it will be checked afterwards whether you were indeed entitled to extra time. There are consequences if it turns out not to be true.
