There are two ways to do the inspection: inspection with support or without support. If you want to do an inspection with support you should notify your programme coordinator at least 7 weeks prior to the inspection date. Inspections without support can be arranged with ICTO within 3 days. Please refer to the flowchart below to quickly determine which type of inspection suits your situation.


Booking

What's the difference between an inspection with or without technical support?

  • Inspections with technical support are similar to digital exams: they are protected by a password on IP-controlled UvA computers, which are prepared by an Assistant Floor Manager (AFM'er). As a result, students cannot take pictures, screenshots or copy questions to their own device. Similar to digital exams, you'll need to inform your programme coordinator at least 7 weeks ahead of time to arrange an inspection with support.

    Inspections without technical support are done on students' own devices without an AFM'er on location. Students can log in from anywhere, so this type of inspection can also be done from home and last multiple days. Simply mail ICTO with the desired timeframe and optional features (comments and answer model) 3 days ahead of time to arrange an inspection without support. We will configure the inspection in TestVision and send you the login instructions for students.

How do I book an inspection with technical support?

  • To arrange an inspection with support, notify your programme coordinator at least 7 weeks prior to the inspection date. The programme coordinator will arrange a room after which ICTO will book the AFM'er.

    The deadlines for booking an inspection with support can be found on this page, under the 'Applying for TestVision' section.

I missed the deadline for booking an inspection with technical support. What can I do?

  • In case you missed the deadline for booking an inspection with technical support, your only option is to book an inspection without technical support or print the inspection.

How do I book an inspection without technical support?

  • Inspections without support can take place on any date, time and location. There is no maximum on how long these inspections can be available, so it could last for multiple days if you wish.

    Simply mail ICTO at least 3 days ahead of time with the desired timeframe and options (comments and answer model) and we will configure the inspection in TestVision. Three days prior to the inspection, you will receive an automatic mail with further information.

I would like to do the inspection offline. How do I print a test for an inspection?

  • Results can either be printed per student or for an entire class. A brief guide on how to do so can be found on this page.


Comments

How do I discuss any comments of students?

  • The option for students to leave comments during the inspection is turned off by default, to prevent student from overloading teachers with questions and comments in TestVision. Nevertheless, it is possible for students to leave comments on the exam, so you can later review them one by one.

    Please mail ICTO if you'd like to make use of this option.

How do I download students' comments in TestVision?

  • To download students' comments go to Sessions in TestVision and navigate to your course. On the right side of the test, select Results. At the top of the test page, click to Downloads, after which you should click Comments by candidates as XML (Excel) to download all comments.


Answer model

How do I add an answer model for students to see during an inspection?

  • You are completely free to decide what the answer model may look like. The ICTO team asks you to do two things: (1) Please save the answer model in PDF format, and (2) email the PDF file to icto-csw@uva.nl at least 3 days before the inspection.

    If you have done so, we will make sure the answer model is included in the inspection. This way students can open the answer model on their computer or laptop during the inspection. If it is not convenient for you to deliver the answer model before this deadline, you can still take it with you on USB or print it out for inspection!

What do students see during an inspection?

  • By default, students see the following during an inspection:

    • Every question.
    • Their own answer to every question.
    • The correct answer (in case of a closed question).
    • The number of points they earned per question.
    • The number of points a question is worth.
    • Feedback that you have entered while grading.
    • Study reference (only if it was filled out prior to the test).

    The options below are disabled by default. Please mail ICTO if you would also like students to see:

    • The answer model for open questions.
    • A button to place comments on the exam.
