I made a few late adjustments to the exam in TestVision. What do I need to do?

If you changed any details in TestVision within one week prior to the exam, please make sure to do the following: publish your changes. If you have added extra questions to the test, please do not forget to actually link question(s) to the exam before publishing your recent changes. You can no longer ask an ICTO employee to perform these steps because the 1 week deadline has passed, the consequence is that you must ensure these steps yourself to avoid mistakes. You can of course reach ICTO for questions but we are not responsible for errors caused by changes after the 1-week deadline nor can we guarantee a timely response.
