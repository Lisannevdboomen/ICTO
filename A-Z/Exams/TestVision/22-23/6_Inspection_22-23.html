There are roughly two ways to do the inspection: inspection with support or without support. If you want to do an inspection with support you should notify your programme coordinator at least 7 weeks prior to the inspection date.

The advantage of an inspection with support is that it increases the possibility of reusing test questions. Without support, students can easily take a picture, screenshot or copy the test questions via laptop or phone. If you decide to print the students' results you can control this more easily, but this can be difficult in large courses with more than 200 students. The advantage of an inspection without support is that you can email us and decide to do the inspection at your own time and place. We will set up the inspection in TestVision and send you the login instructions for students.

How do I book an inspection with technical support?

If you book an inspection with technical support, a location with UvA laptops or desktops will be provided. Additionally Assistant Floor Managers will be present. They are responsible for preparing and technically supporting the inspection for you.

If you'd like to do an inspection with support, you need to notify your programme coordinator way in advance (at least 7 weeks prior to the inspection date). He or she will then also notify ICTO, after which we'll book the technical support for the inspection. The deadlines for booking an inspection with support can be found on this page (subsection: Applying for TestVision).

I missed the deadline for booking an inspection with technical support. What now?

In case you missed the deadline for booking an inspection with technical support, your only option is to book an inspection without support. If you you'd like to do the test inspection at very short notice and ICTO cannot arrange for the inspection to be accessible on TestVision, you could always choose to print the inspection.

How do I book an inspection without technical support?

In case of an inspection without support, teachers can choose their own day, time and location (optional), and they should communicate this information with ICTO via email. It does not matter how long or when you'd like the inspection to be. ICTO puts the inspection in an overview and arranges that the inspection is accessible by students at the desired time. Three days before this moment, the teacher will receive an automatic mail with more instruction and information.

Importantly, location does not matter in case of an inspection without support, as it works on a 'bring your own device' basis. You can do the inspection online or you can choose to sit somewhere with your students. However, it is also possible for teachers to print the test for the inspection.

How do I add an answer model for my students to see during an inspection?

For inspection, it is important to ask yourself whether an answer model is needed for students. If, for example, you have created an exam with a number of open questions, it is useful for students to see during the inspection how the assessment is done.

You are completely free to decide what the answer model may look like. The ICTO team asks you to do two things: (1) Please save the answer model in PDF format, and (2) email the PDF file to icto-csw@uva.nl at least 3 days before the inspection.

If you have done so, we will make sure the answer model is included in the inspection. This way students can open the answer model on their computer or laptop during the inspection. If it is not convenient for you to deliver the answer model before this deadline, you can still take it with you on USB or print it out for inspection!

How do I discuss any comments of students?

It is important to ask yourself whether you would like students to enter any comments they might have in TestVision. It is also possible for you to discuss any questions or comments with students on the spot. During an inspection, the option to enter comments is turned off by default. This way, a teacher is not overloaded with questions and comments from students. A big advantage of turning the option on, however, is that a teacher is then able to download all comments from TestVision.

Please send an email to icto-csw@uva.nl if you'd like to make use of this option.

How do I download students' comments in TestVision?

To download students' comments go to Sessions in TestVision and select the relevant folder. On the right side of the test, select Results. At the top of the test page, you can go to Downloads, after which you should click 'Comments by candidates as XML (Excel)'
