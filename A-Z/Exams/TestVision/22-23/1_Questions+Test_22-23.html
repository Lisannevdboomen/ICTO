Teachers are solely responsible for modifying the questions in TestVision. Questions can be changed by teachers until the last moment. A request for adding new questions to the test or changing test settings can be sent to ICTO up to one week before the start of the test. After this deadline, teachers can also do these things themselves with the help of a detailed instructions.

How can I edit a question?

Go to TestVision and login via SURFconext. Click on the menu Questions and select the right folder of the course on the left. If you don't have access to your course yet, please contact ICTO.

  • Double-click the question you'd like to edit. You can write down the question text in the input field beneath 'Stem'.
  • In the menu on the right, you can adjust the question properties: for instance, the number of points, the option to randomize the order in which answer options are shown (MC questions), and the possibility to show a word count (open questions).
  • Beware! If you have open questions, you will always have to fill in grading criteria at the bottom of the page. You can also use multiple criteria (a check-up model) for subsections that make up an essay question.

Don't forget to save your changes by clicking on Save at the top of the screen.

How can I change the name of a question?

Go to TestVision and login via SURFconext. Click on the menu Questions and select the right folder of the course on the left. If you don't have access to your course yet, please contact ICTO.

Go to the question you'd like to rename and right click on it. Select Rename. You can also select the question and press w on your keyboard.

How can I add a question?

Go to TestVision and login via SURFconext. Click on the menu Questions and select the right folder of the course on the left. If you don't have access to your course yet, please contact ICTO.

Select +New in the top left corner. You can also press n on your keyboard. A new question will open. Choose the type of question you want to create at the top of the screen. On this page, you can read more about the available question types.

What kind of question types can I use?

In the settings at the top of each question, you can choose the question type. Generally, multiple choice and open questions are used most often. On the following page, you can find all other question types that are available.
