Starting this academic year, it is again possible to offer digital exams on location. If you are going to create a digital exam that will be administered on site, please note that you can only use TestVision for this purpose. All other forms of exams (such as assignments, take home exams, and so on) can be done through Canvas. Check out our instructions below to learn more about the process of creating an exam with TestVision.

I want to use TestVision

  • Notify your programme coordinator as early as possible.
    • The programme coordinators will schedule the exam to the roster. Our team must book in all details according to the roster at least 7 weeks before the start of the test/retake/inspection.

  • Take a look at our instructions
    • Get to know TestVision in 6 steps.
    • Click on the tiles above to view the instructions.
    • Take a look at our deadlines on the right.

  • Get in contact with ICTO
    • If you would like to schedule a short intake or meeting to get to know TestVision.
    • If you would like to gain access to your course folders in TestVision (if we haven't done so already).

Deadlines

  • 7 workdays prior to the exam
    • Step 1, Step 2, and Step 3
  • Moment of examination
    • Step 4
  • Assessment and Inspection
    • Step 5 and Step 6
