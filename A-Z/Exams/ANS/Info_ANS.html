Introducing...

In the 2023-2024 academic year, the UvA will make a transition to a new software system for online testing. This is the outcome of a statutory European tender. Two suppliers applied for this tender. After an extensive evaluation, Ans Exam came out on top. The current supplier, TestVision, came in second. This means that all UvA faculties will switch to Ans Exam in phases in the coming academic year.

With Ans Exam, the UvA acquires an innovative partner for the long term with a contract of at least 6 years with an extension option to a maximum of 15 years. This long term is the basis for a stable service and cooperation. Ans Exam has extensive experience in academic education with digital testing. The continuous development and innovation of Ans Exam will be partly based on the specific needs of the UvA.

The implementation of the new digital testing software will take place in phases during the 2023-2024 academic year. This will be done in close cooperation with the faculty expert teams. In terms of pace and timing, the specific situation of each faculty or program will be taken into account. Staff and faculty can count on maximum support and (technical) assistance from ICTO.

Of course, we'll keep you updated. For now, you can check out our extensive TestVision instructions.
