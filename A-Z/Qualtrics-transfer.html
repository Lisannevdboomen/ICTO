The UvA has had a new, UvA-wide Qualtrics license since last summer (2020). FMG researchers and teachers will also have to work with this license in their course.

When you create a new survey, you no longer use the "old" FMG account (as uvacommscience.eu.qualtrics.com/en), but the new UvA license (Links to an external site.). This is accessible to all employees and students of the UvA.
For more information, click here.

How to move surveys from your account in the "old" Qualtrics brand to the "new" Qualtrics brand

1. Find out which process is required to move your survey(s) by going through the decision tree below
 

Click here to open image in a new window
2. Navigate to the instructions...
 
  • Manual Move (do it yourself)

    • Navigate to the instructions for a Manual Move by clicking the link above and get started right away. With this method, you avoid having to wait 2-3 weeks for Qualtrics Support to move your survey(s).


  • User Move Process

    • The User Move Process must be performed by Qualtrics Support and takes approximately 2-3 weeks.


    Follow the steps below:

    1. Make sure all your active surveys are paused.
    2. Make sure to inform those who have shared a survey with you about the fact that they need to share the survey(s) with you again in the new Qualtrics brand. If you don't need access to these surveys anymore, this is not required.

      If someone has shared surveys with you, this collaboration will be broken when your account is moved. This is because collaboration is linked to email addresses, and the account you move everything to will most likely have a different email address.
      However, all you have to do to regain access to these surveys is ask whoever shared them with you to share them again with your new email address.

    3. Check if any of the 'additional concerns' on the instructions page apply to you
    4. Get in touch with ICTO-SW via email and put in a request for a User Move Process

      E: icto-csw@uva.nl
