    How to submit final grades to SIS

Get Ready

   Take your mobile phone, you'll need it for Two Factor Authentication (2FA) (view more)

    Remove any pop-up blockers present in your URL search bar (view more)

 

Contact

If you don't see a red Submit grades button, please get in touch with your Programme Coordinator or Manager. They can give you the 'Examiner' rights in SIS. Please note that only the coördinator of the course can submit grades to SIS. 

If you have successfully submitted your grades to SIS, but there is an issue of some sort or if you have a question about your grades in SIS, please get in touch with the Registrar's office (gradeadmin-sw@uva.nl).

 

Summary of symbols

These symbols may appear after you're done or during the progress:

 =  You cannot transfer grades for this student

 =  This student is not enrolled in SIS for this course.

=  This grade was imported from a file or entered or modified manually.

= This grade has been transferred to SIS but has not yet arrived in SIS. In some cases, this icon indicates that the grade list has not been signed yet. You can find the unsigned list at the bottom right in the Final Grades menu. Click on the list and complete the session by clicking on "Sign grade list using two-factor authentication"

= The final grade has been successfully transferred to SIS.

 = The resit grade has been successfully transferred to SIS.

 = The grade is currently being transferred to SIS.

= Unfortunately, an error occurred during the transfer. You don't have to act on this error, the administration office will automatically receive a message and they will contact you if anything seems wrong. In most cases, this icon only indicates errors that are excepted

Instructions

1. Open Final Grades from the course menu
 

Wait for it to load, it may take some time.

2.

Select for whom you want to submit grades at Grade list for      

 

If you are the examiner of the whole course, select All students

If you are the examiner of a certain section, select Section

If you would like to select certain students, then use the red boxes in front of students' names

 
3. Click the Submit grades button at the right
   
4. Select Final grade from the dropdown box at the top and press Submit
 

 

 
5. A pop-up window will open where you can start the 2FA process
 

Please make sure your browser enables pop-ups. 
6.

Click on Sign grade list using two-factor authentication
 

   
7. Two-factor authentication
 

First of all, you will be asked to select using your institution, in our case: UvAUvAnetID

 

Then, you can two-factor authenticate, using tiqr, sms or ubikey (however you have set this up in the past) and follow the steps that are provided.

 
8.

Confirmation and optional download of grade list
 

You will receive a message saying:

This grade list has been signed and confirmed.

 

 

If desired, download the grade list in the form of a PDF file.
9.

Click to the Final grades button menu to reload the page
 

Send corrections to SiS

1. Go to the Final Grades menu
 
2. Click on Enter correction at the right side and select Correction for final grade
 

 
3. Navigate to the student and then click on the + icon located in the Correction column
 

Note: this column appears after completing step 2.

 
4. Now enter the final grade

 
5. When you're done, push the grade to SiS by clicking on the Submit grades
 

Note: you are in the so-called "correction mode" after performing step 2. This will affect the process of submitting grades to SiS, meaning you'll only push the newly corrected grade(s). Therefore, we advise you to do the above steps for all corrections in one go.
