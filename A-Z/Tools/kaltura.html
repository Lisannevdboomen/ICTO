Welcome to our Kaltura page. Here you can find necessary information about our Video Web Editing Tool. Kaltura is the University of Amsterdam's current video platform. Kaltura works very well within Canvas. As a teacher you can use Kaltura to smoothly offer videos within your courses. It is also possible, for example, to make annotations on videos or to put together a video quiz. 

Live support
From every Monday till Friday, there is a live support section at video.uva.nl from 11:00 until 12:00 (click here). Also, there are online workshops on Tuesday from 10:00 till 11:00 (click here).
