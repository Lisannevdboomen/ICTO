Request for weblecture recordings

    • Teachers can request one or several recordings via http://webcollegeplanner.uva.nl/.
    • By clicking on this link you can view the locations (lecture halls) that are equipped for recordings. 
    • After completing the request you will receive a URL-link from the web lecture services (Dienst Webcollege) that contains all past and futures recordings.

Livestream

    • It is also possible to create a livestream of your lectures and share it with students. You can request the livestream function by going to http://webcollegeplanner.uva.nl/

Past recordings

    • Given the circumstances, we've provided a database for teachers containing all weblectures that were recorded between 2018 and 2020. You can view the lectures here.

 

For more questions about weblectures, please contact webcolleges@uva.nl (T: 020 525 2800)

Finding the right format for your classes

    • While we are most certainly excited for our on-campus days to return, realistically, we also know that there are likely to be some “post-pandemic” situations that necessitate some flexibility in teaching. The FMG-Hybrid Committee has worked with the TLC, ICTO, and FS-BOL to ensure that you have the information and technology you need to make flexible teaching accessible to you. Since we know that classes, content, and teachers are different, the options are also quite varied. With that in mind, on this website, you can find flexible teaching options grouped into three general formats.

OWL

    • The OWL is a device that provides online students a 360-degree viewing experience of the classroom (via a 360-degree camera) and allows students to hear the lecturer as it also functions as in-classroom microphone.
    • Prior to the class, teachers can request to use the OWLS by emailing servicedesk-fs@uva.nl (020 525) 1403 and/or working with their teaching planner. The device will be brought to the classroom at the scheduled time.
    • More Information offered by the Audio Visual Service of the REC.

BRIO

    • The Brio is a webcam with an exceptionally good microphone, which has been installed in the lecture halls and which can be reserved for use in a seminar room from the REC Facility Services.
    • Start-up support is available on request or ad-hoc in case of AV issues via the Facility Service Desk, (020 525) 1403 or servicedesk-fs@uva.nl
    • More Information offered by the Audio Visual Service of the REC.

Hybrid Learning Theatre

    • With professional audiovisual support and a unique atmosphere, this space offers an exceptional experience for hybrid teaching.
    • Time slots are available every day from 08:30-22:00. It is not only possible to have lectures in the theater but also Q&A sessions, seminars/tutorials, student presentations or other types of activities. There can be 13 students invited for physical attendance at a time but it is not mandatory.
    • More information can be found here.

Zoom

Kaltura (MyMedia)

    • Kaltura is a modern video platform. It is a durable state-of-the-art solution for or creating video or audio by teachers, supporters and students. Kaltura works very well with Canvas, you can find it under the menu 'MyMedia'. As a teacher you can easily offer videos within your course, the storage of videos has no effect on course memory. More information can be found here.
