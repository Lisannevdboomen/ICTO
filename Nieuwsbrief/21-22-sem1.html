Here's what's new in Semester 1

Final Grades

  • Starting this academic year, there has been added a new menu to Canvas called the Final Grades menu. Teachers are required to use this new menu to push final grades from Canvas to SIS. More information about the implementation of Final Grades can be found here. To get started with Final Grades, use our Final Grades manual. 


Online & Hybrid tools

  • Visit ICTO's page about Online and Hybrid tools for all ins and outs on the use of weblectures (use the webcollegeplanner to request recordings), livestreaming devices (BRIO, OWL or Hybrid Learning Theatre), WooClap (increase student engagement by presenting live polls during your lectures), and Office365 (use MS Teams to collaborate with UvA-colleagues or OneDrive to store your files).


Digital Exams

  • There are two ways to create online exams: with TestVision or with Canvas. If you are going to create an online exam that will be administered on site, please note that you can only use TestVision for this purpose. Contact your study programme coordinator or manager to discuss which option(s) apply to your study programme.
  • About TestVision: due to the current hectique, we ask teachers to be as self-sufficient as possible. The deadline to have everything ready in TestVision is seven days before the start of the exam. Please prepare your exam on time so we can address unforeseen issues with less stress. If stuck, check our TestVision Manual or get in contact with ICTO by email or book an appointment


New for students

  • In May 2021 there has been a change in email address and Canvas notifications for students, more information for students can be found here. 
  • From this academic year onward, the UvA does not provide a general Atlas.ti license code anymore. To get a license code for Atlas.ti, students are required to fill out the form on this page.

 

New to teaching at the UvA? 
 

  • Optimize your Canvas course by using our course checklist.
  • Ouriginal is the default plagiarism checker for Canvas assignments (previously named 'Urkund') and has replaced Turnitin since the start of last year. More information can be found in our Ouriginal manual.
  • Qualtrics has slowly been migrating from faculty-specific licenses to the UvA-wide Qualtrics license. When creating a new survey, make sure to log in to the new Qualtrics environment. More information on the transition to the new Qualtrics license can be found here.
  • Navigate to the 'MyMedia' button in your Canvas menu on the left to view videos in your own Kaltura library.
  • Is your Canvas course free from Copyright infringement? Head over to the Copyright & Canvas manual to find more information on copyright regulations or to access copyright-free media databases.
  • New to the UvA and doing a digital examination in TestVision? Email ICTO and if there are enough applicants, we can set up a TestVision workshop.
