MyMedia can be used to transcribe your audio or video files, making sure that you won't have to spend your time typing out transcripts word for word. You can specify whether your audio or video file contains sensitive information by using the Confedential Media setting. That way, you can make sure your files are safely stored and automatically deleted after 183 days. You can transcribe your files directly in My Media on Canvas or on the video.uva.nl website.

Please keep in mind that this tool is not originally intended for transcribing, as it is a video platform, so it will probably not work as well as a real transcribing tool. On the other hand, MyMedia (Kaltura) is a UvA app, so it is safer to use this tool than to run sensitive material such as interviews through random apps from the internet.


1. Go to Canvas and click the My Media button on the left
 


2. Upload your video or audio file by clicking Add New > Media Upload and select your file
 

3. Fill out a Name and Description
 

4. Select YES for the Confidential Media option
  If your video or audio file contains any sensitive information, this setting makes sure the file is automatically deleted after 183 days.


5. Select a Language and your Department
 


6. Make sure that the Publishing Status is set to Private
 


7. Click the Save button
 


8. A pop-up message with information about confidential media entries will appear. Click Confirm
 


9. Click on Go to media
 


10. Below your video/audio file, click on Actions and select the Caption & Enrichment option
 


11. Select the language corresponding with your audio (Dutch or English) and click the Submit button
 


It may take a while for your audio transcription to be generated. Our advice is to keep yourself busy with something else in the meantime.
