Instead of sharing a URL link, you can retrieve the recordings and/or livestream from the WebcollegeDienst in Canvas and embed them in a page. By doing this, students aren't able to share lectures with other students outside of the course. If you embed the video within an Assignment, you can add additional viewing restrictions. Follow the instructions below to see what option suits best for you.

How to embed weblectures in Canvas


1. Go to the page or assignment where you want to add the video and click Edit.
  With an Assignment you can add two additional restrictions:
  • Automatically set a specific time limit within which the lecture can be viewed
  • Determine which students or sections ("werkgroepen") can view the lecture
2. Click on the Mediasite icon (4 orange dots).
 


3. Choose your preferred option.
 

Pesentation    share one particular lecture
Students aren't able to share the weblecture with other students who aren't enrolled to the course.
  • You can only find and add recordings after the lecture is done
  • The livestream link will appear 6 days in advance

Collection    share all lectures at once
It's possible for students to share the weblecture with other students with a UvAnetID.
  • You can find and add the collection with recordings in advance, after the lecture is done the recording will appear in the list.
  • The livestream collection will appear 6 days in advance
4. Search for the specific weblecture or collection by its subject name or code.
 


5. Click on the button Add to.. next to the video and then select Player Only.
  The 'Player Only' option will embed the video.


6. Once you're done, click on Save & Publish at the bottom of the page.
  Or click 'Save' if you do not want the video to be published yet.
