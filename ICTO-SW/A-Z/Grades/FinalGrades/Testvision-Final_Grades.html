Recently, it has become a lot easier to export students’ results from TestVision directly to Final Grades in Canvas. This means: no more hassle with importing and exporting Excel files, which will save you not only a lot of time but also a lot of effort. This page describes the steps you need to go through to do this.

What does this look like in TestVision?

Previously, the grade calculation in TestVision allowed you to see grades obtained by your students. Because of the direct link between TestVision and Canvas, this calculation is no longer done in TestVision, but in Final Grades itself.

This means you’ll only be able to see the percentages students have achieved. These percentages are a direct reflection of the points obtained compared to the total number of points.

In the image on the right, you can see how this looks in TestVision, after you have validated the results.

How do I see these results?

1. Go to Sessions in TestVision and select the relevant one

2. Click on Results at the right of the relevant session

How do I add the TestVision results to Final Grades?
1. Go to the Final Grades menu and open the grade Calculation Setup
 

Can't see this course menu?

  1. Go to Settings at the bottom of your course menu

  2. Click on the tab Navigation at the top

  3. Scroll down until you reach the Final Grades menu. Enable this menu by clicking on the icon with three dots or by dragging and drop this menu to the top.

  4. Click on Save at the bottom of the page
2. Click on the top right button Add partial result and select Existing assignment
 


3. A new window will pop-up. Select the relevant item(s)
 


4. Canvas will ask if you want a new calculated component. Press No
 



5. Now you will see your assignments lined up in the Grade Calculation Settings
