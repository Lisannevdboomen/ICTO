TestVision

 

 
  • TestVision
  • Digital examination software

TestVision is a digital examination application for exams and depends more on ICTO. Compared to creating exams on Canvas, you will need a little more time to learn how to work with TestVision, but there are certainly more options and settings at your disposal to create strong exams and to review and adjust the quality of questions. Visit our TestVision page to become familiar with the overall creation process, and when you're ready, contact icto if you'd like to create an exam with TestVision.

Ans

 

 
  
  • Ans Exam
  • Digital examination software

In the academic year 2023-2024 the UvA will transition to a new software system for digital exams. This is the result of a statuatory European tender. Two suppliers had registered for this tender. After an extensive evaluation, Ans emerged as the best choice. The current supplier, TestVision, came in second. This means that all faculties of the UvA will gradually switch to Ans during the upcoming academic year.
