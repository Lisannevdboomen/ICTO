The most frequently asked questions by students with regards to the TestVision digital examination process are listed below. It's recommended to take a glance at all questions to familiarize yourself with the examination process.


Logging in

How do I log in to TestVision for an exam?

  • Please refer to this instruction. Please note that you need your UvAnetID and password to log in, so make sure to remember these ahead of the exam. Forgot your UvAnetID or password? Click here.

How do I log in to TestVision for an inspection?

  • Please refer to this instruction. Please note that you need your UvAnetID and password to log in, so make sure to remember these ahead of the exam. Forgot your UvAnetID or password? Click here.


Arrival and departure times

What happens if I'm later? Can I nevertheless start the exam?

  • If you are less than 30 minutes late, you can still start the exam, but you might not receive full time anymore depending on the teacher's decision. If you arrive late by 30 minutes or more, you are no longer allowed to enter the examination room and have automatically failed the exam.

When am I allowed to leave the examination room?

  • You're only allowed to leave the room after 30 minutes, even if you finish earlier.


Materials

What do I need to bring to the exam?

  • To take part in the exam, you'll need to be able to identify yourself with a valid proof of registration (e.g. your UvA pass, ID, passport or driving license) with a recent passport photo.

    Make sure you know your UvAnetID and password by heart to log in on TestVision. Forgot your UvAnetID or password? Click here.

    If the teacher decides to provide scrap paper, you will need to bring your own pen(s). Bags will have to be put in lockers in bigger exam locations.

Can I bring my bag?

  • Yes. You are required to store your bag inside a locker in large examination locations. In smaller examination locations, you're allowed to take your bag inside to your desk unless stated otherwise by the teacher and/or staff.


Extra time

I have a disability and need extra time for the exam. What should I do?

  • You must request extra time at least 6 weeks prior to the exam. Once your request has been approved, you will automatically receive extra time for the upcoming TestVision exams for as long as your grant lasts. Students can request extra time (and other special facilities) via this link.

What if I do not have extra time during the exam even though I am entitled to it?

  • You can see the (remaining) amount of time in TestVision at all times: in the instruction, on the timer below every question and in the on-screen notification 5 minutes before the exam ends. If you believe you have not been granted the appropriate amount of time, please raise your hand as soon as possible to inform the teacher or staff. If approved by the teacher, the staff will provide you extra time on the spot. Whether the extra time was granted rightfully will be evaluated afterwards. If you have already handed in the exam or time has run out, you will no longer be granted extra time, so make sure to double check the timer in TestVision at the very start of (or during) the exam.

    For future exams, make sure to apply for extra time as soon as possible via this link.
