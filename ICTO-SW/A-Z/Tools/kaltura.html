Kaltura is the University of Amsterdam's current video platform. Kaltura is used by lecturers, support staff and students to upload and/or create video or audio. Kaltura is compatible with Canvas, making it easy for lecturers to integrate video into their courses. You can access Kaltura either via video.uva.nl or through My Media in Canvas.

Uploading

Should I use My Media in Canvas or video.uva.nl?

  • Both links are gateways to UvA's Kaltura platform, across which all media is shared. Thus, if you upload a video on one of the two, you will be able to view and edit it in the other. While all content is shared, there's a few small differences in functionality between the gateways, which are explained below. As a rule of thumb, we recommend to use My Media in Canvas, unless you want to share things outside of your Canvas course.

    The exact difference in functionality are listed below:

    • On video.uva.nl you can create a shareable link of your video. You cannot do this from Canvas.
    • In Canvas' My Media, you can publish your video to the Media Gallery of your Canvas course. You cannot publish videos to the Media Gallery of your Canvas course from video.uva.nl.
    • On video.uva.nl, you can create and add videos to your channel(s). Your channel is akin to the Media Gallery of a Canvas course, but has a couple of more options with regards to access, sharing and posting of videos.

I have an existing video. How do I upload it to Kaltura?

  • Simply click Add new > Media Upload to select a file from your device. You can also select Add new > YouTube to upload a YouTube video to Kaltura.

I want to create a video. Can I use Kaltura?

  • You can also create your own videos using Kaltura Capture, Kaltura's proprietary software. Learn how to download and use Kaltura Capture on the UvA's website.


Sharing

How do I insert (embed) a video in my Canvas course?

    1. To embed a video in Canvas, simply click the three dots and the Kaltura icon.
    2. An overview of your Kaltura videos will show. Click Embed to insert the video in your Canvas page.

     

    Note: Don't see your video? You'll have to upload it to Kaltura first!

     

Can I share Kaltura videos with people outside of the UvA?

  • Yes, you can share an individual video via a link or your entire channel with people outside of the UvA (i.e. people that do not have a UvA-mail).

    • To share an invidiual video, go to video.uva.nl and upload your media by clicking + Add new at the top right of the window. If you've already uploaded your video, go to My Media (on video.uva.nl) and click Edit instead. Near the bottom of the options, select Unlisted under 'Publishing Status' and click Save.
    • To share your channel, click on My Channels on video.uva.nl. Go to the channel you wish to open up for everyone and click on Edit (top right). Select Public, Restricted under 'Privacy' and click Save.

Assignments

How do I create an assignment for which students hand in a video?

  • A Kaltura video assignment is created as follows:

    1. Go to your Canvas course and create an assignment by clicking + Assignment.
    2. Under 'Submission type' select Online > Text entry.
    3. Fill out the rest of your assignment details and click Save at the bottom of the page.
    4. Students can hand in their video assignment by clicking the three dots and selecting the Kaltura icon (see image below).

    Please refer to the UvA's instruction for more details or contact us.

    Note: Make sure students use Kaltura to submit their work and not the built-in Canvas media option! If too many students use the built-in media option, your course's storage might exceed the allowed maximum and students will not be able to hand anything at all.

Is there a page with Kaltura instructions accessible for students?

  • Yes, you can direct your students to the UvA's official documentation. Specific topics:

    • How do I submit my assignment using Kaltura?
    • How do I record and edit a video using Kaltura Capture?
