Friday at the Attic

Every other Friday we have the amazing opportunity to be at the attic of ‘Het Limmickhof’, a building at the Amstelhoven complex: Computational Social Science’s Friday at the Attic.
Advanced Reading Group

The advanced reading group focuses on theory and complex topics in computational social science. We will balance theoretical readings in political science and sociology with cutting-edge research in computational social science.
Career Development

You, as a Computational Social Science student, will start your career development from Day 1 in the programme. Throughout the courses you will further specialise and professionalise your academic knowledge and skills and you will expand your network when working with external partners.
Student Wellbeing

The UvA offers many different services for students regarding their wellbeing. Are you looking for guidance, help or support to improve your wellbeing, these services could be helpful. Not sure which path to take or who to contact? Take a look at the UvA wellbeing infographic or make an appointment with the studyadviser.
Examinations Board

The Examinations Board has various legal duties and powers in relation to the quality of assessments, certification, exemptions and the handling of fraud and plagiarism. The Board reviews, amongst other, requests for exemptions, and approval of electives and extracurricular courses.
Programme Committee

The Programme Committee (PC) is a representative advisory body in which students and teaching staff discuss the quality and content of education.
