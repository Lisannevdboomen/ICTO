Our team

ICTO Social Sciences consists of an enthusiastic team of student-assistants and regular employees. The student-assistants have a variable schedule of 16 hours per week as they combine their studies and ICTO.

You can find us at REC B7.08, and you’re free to walk in. Usually there is someone available at the office from Mondays to Thursdays. Of course, you can also e-mail us at icto-csw@uva.nl. We try our best to respond to your messages as soon as possible! If you’d like to make an appointment with the team, you can do so via the booking form at the bottom of this page. An appointment can take place online or at the office.
