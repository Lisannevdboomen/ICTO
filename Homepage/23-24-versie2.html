Persuall info page

Have you checked out our new Perusall information page yet? Perusall enables your students to collaboratively annotate documents of your choice. Its intended use is to stimulate students to complete the required reading before lectures, so that you can continue discussions or address questions during the lecture itself.

Our recently updated information page explains more about how to get started with this tool through a handy roadmap and useful tips & tricks. Another great addition to the page is the 'live example’. This shows you an example of the student view of a Perusall assignment.

Check it out and don't hesitate to contact ICTO if you need more support or would like to brainstorm about the possibilities in the tool.
